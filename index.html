I Knew You Would Forgive Me 💕

Thank you for giving me another chance and for having such a soft and beautiful heart. I know I may not always be perfect, and sometimes I make mistakes that hurt you without meaning to. But one thing I never want you to doubt is how important you are to me. Your smile brings peace to my chaos, and your presence makes even ordinary days feel special. I truly miss the moments when we laugh together without worries, and I hope we can create even more beautiful memories ahead. You mean more to me than words can fully explain. I promise to appreciate you more, listen better, and protect the happiness we share together. Thank you for forgiving me, for staying, and for being the sweetest part of my life. 💖
